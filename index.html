ABOUT


Urban Matrix is an art and architectural design studio based in Chandrapur. Architect Sachi Ambare, the principal architect of the studio graduated from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur in 2018.

Keenly interested in visual art and later developing the fascination of interconnectedness of the functionality and aesthetics, the architect aims to provide the architectural consultancy with client's priority and well informed service from the studio.
